Oshinowo Kolade Adekunle

Born in the late 1940s, Oshinowo Kolade Adekunle hails from Ibadan, Nigeria

  • Ibadan Grammar School, Ibadan, (1964-1966)
  • Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, (1968-1972)
